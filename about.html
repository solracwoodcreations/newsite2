About the artisan

Hi, I’m [Your Name].

I’m the founder and sole artisan behind Opal Woodgrain. Every piece you see here is designed, crafted, and finished by my hands.

At Opal Woodgrain, every creation is a testament to artistry, care, and individuality. I transform reclaimed and donated hardwoods into functional, elegant pieces that are as purposeful as they are beautiful. Each design is born from my hands, my imagination, and a lifetime of creative exploration.

Where It Began

My love for design began in childhood. In second grade, I entered a school project contest using glued toothpicks. While most children created flat designs, I built mine in three dimensions. That piece earned 2nd place at the New Mexico State Fair, hinting at a lifelong path of innovation and creativity.

The Craftsmanship Journey

Woodworking became a passion in middle school. I learned to shape, carve, and bring ideas to life, and my teachers quickly recognized a natural affinity with tools and materials.

As an adult, I honed precision, problem-solving, and craftsmanship in the construction industry — metal framing, drywall, and painting — learning skills that would later define my approach to woodwork.

Resilience & Return

A life-altering car accident could have ended my hands-on journey. Doctors said I might never walk again. Against all odds, I returned to movement, and with it, the craft I love.

Today, every piece I create is designed, carved, and finished by me — no assembly lines, no factories, just one artist bringing ideas to life.

What I Create

From wall-mounted coffee stations to innovative multipurpose bag clips, key holders, travel trays, candleholders, and phone stands, each creation blends function with refined design.

I invent solutions that are elegant, practical, and entirely unique — like my patented bag clips that work on any bag, not just chips.

Beyond Wood

Beyond wood, I have taught myself the digital craft: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I built this website from scratch using only my iPhone XR and GitHub.

Opal Woodgrain represents the intersection of lifelong creativity, resilience, and skill — a brand where every object tells a story, and every story begins with my hands.
